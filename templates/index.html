How to use?
Click on the choose files button.
Click on the "upload" button to upload the video.
The video will be uploaded and you will see a preview of it.
Click on the "Process" button to start blurring the faces in the video.
Once the processing is complete, you will see a new video with the faces blurred.
Click on the "Download" button to download the blurred video.
